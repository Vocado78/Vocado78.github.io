Hello, my name is Ingrid and I'm a Front-end Developer based in Stockholm.

About me

Little did I know that the change I desired could be found in coding. In September of 2016 I followed some friends' advice and joined a free, online coding curriculum to try out some front-end coding. I loved it! The problem-solving mindset, the creativity it requires, and the rapid progress in the technology industry really suited me. It turned out to be such a perfect match that I left a stable job in contracts management at a pharmaceutical company in April of 2017 to learn and practise front-end development full-time.

When I'm not coding I'm likely seeing my friends or family, out running, solving crossword puzzles, reading about sports or current affairs, or out on a hike with my partner. Oh and in most cases you probably find me accompanied by a mug of tea.

Are you looking to hire? Send me a message! I'd love to see how I can contribute.

What I do

My goal is to build functional, fast, and user-friendly websites and apps. Although there are tonnes of great libraries out there I like to keep things simple and not use more than what a project requires. The easiest and quickest option at the time is not always the best in the long run. I believe this approach, together with well-structured code, facilitate easier maintenance of a project. Below you can find my latest work.
